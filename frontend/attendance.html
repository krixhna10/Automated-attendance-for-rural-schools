📋 How It Works

  1. Click "Start Attendance" to activate the camera
  2. Students stand in front of the camera one by one
  3. System automatically recognizes faces and marks attendance
  4. Each student can only be marked present once per day
  5. View the attendance list below in real-time
💡 Tip: Ensure good lighting and students face the camera directly for accurate recognition.
